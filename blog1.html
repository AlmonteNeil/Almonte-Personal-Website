Introduction

My name is Neil Almonte, productivity tools play a big role in helping me manage my daily tasks or school requirements. One tool that I will not leave behind is Canva because, in making or designing presentations or reports, it makes design easy even if you do not have strong graphic design skills. Another tool that I cannot leave behind is Google Workspace, especially Google Docs and Google Drive, because they allow me to work from anywhere and collaborate with classmates for free and very easily.

Main Content

How AI Is Impacting IT Lives Today

There are many changes being brought about for current IT students and professionals by Artificial Intelligence. Through the use of chatbots and code suggestion systems, Artificial Intelligence is improving the processes of problem-solving and learning. For a learner like me, I consider AI as a helper as well as a challenge because while it makes work easier, it must be used carefully. It encourages IT students to focus on logic rather than pure memorization.

The Programming Language That Challenges Me the Most

Each student in the IT field has to face programming languages that seem difficult at the beginning. For me, a difficult language requires strict syntax and strong logical thinking, like C++ or Java. Such languages can be frustrating because a tiny mistake can cause numerous difficult-to-locate errors. However, such challenges consolidate my problem-solving skills and teach me to be more patient. As time goes by, I become more confident with every struggle.

Coding vs Typing: The Truth About IT Programming

Many believe that programming means only typing fast, but from my experience, it involves much more. Programming is about understanding logic, planning, and the step-by-step breakdown of problems. Typing is only a minor part of the entire process, while the breakdown of problems takes most of the time. This experience taught me that a programmer does not have to be fast, but clear-minded.

Conclusion

Technology impacts the lives of IT students in a number of ways, whether it be the tools we apply, the skills we learn, or the applications we develop. Productivity suites allow us to remain organized, AI changes how we learn, and coding challenges help us improve. The most important concept is adaptability, as technology goes beyond simple tools—it is a mindset.
